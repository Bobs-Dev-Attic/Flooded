HOSE

FLOODED

You are trapped underground. Blast the dirt with your water hose, carve tunnels in 8 directions, dodge the hazards, and ride the rising water to the surface — before it drowns you.

  • Move / Jump — left circle pad (or A / D / W)
  • Aim & spray hose — right ring, hold a direction (or hold Mouse / Arrow keys)
  • Water drains through drains — but clogged ones make it rise
  • Watch for rocks, gas, rodents & hidden cavities
  • Submerged too long → you run out of AIR
